4 New Pythondev VM

4.1 This is the info on whether to move or copy vm

uuid.action = "create" generate new uuid this is this the "I copied the VM" answer generates new MAC for ethernet*-devices use for first boot of copied or cloned VMS uuid.action = "keep" keep uuid this is this the "I moved the VM" answer keeps existing MAC use when you often move your VM

to avoid activation issues use "I moved it" or uuid.action = "keep" the other option generates a new MAC address and therefor is perceived as a serious hardware change
